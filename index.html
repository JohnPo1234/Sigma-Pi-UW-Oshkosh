Sigma Pi · Gamma‑Mu Chapter at the University of Wisconsin–Oshkosh.

Sigma Pi is a national fraternity founded in 1897, with the Gamma‑Mu Chapter established at UW Oshkosh. The chapter is built on scholarship, leadership, service, and brotherhood—values that carry directly into how we work with local businesses.

Who we are

Nationally, Sigma Pi was founded on February 26, 1897, and has grown into a fraternity focused on advancing scholarship, leadership, and character. At UW Oshkosh, the Gamma‑Mu Chapter was chartered in the 1980s and remains an active part of the fraternity community.

Our colors include lavender and white with a gold auxiliary, which you’ll see reflected in this site’s palette. The chapter participates in philanthropy, campus leadership, and service—especially through causes like the Amazing Day Foundation, which focuses on mental health awareness and suicide prevention.

Questions

Frequently asked questions.

A quick overview of how we work with small businesses, what to expect, and how flexible the packages are.

How far in advance should we book?
During the academic year, we recommend reaching out 2–3 weeks before you’d like to film so we can align on schedules, locations, and weather considerations.
Can we customize a package?
Yes. The listed packages are starting points. We can adjust the number of videos, filming locations, and usage rights to match your goals and budget.
Do you provide on‑camera talent?
For UGC, we can use members of Sigma Pi or work with your staff/customers. For street interviews, we typically approach real people in public spaces with clear prompts and consent.
What if we’ve never done video before?
That’s common. We’ll help you choose a package, define a simple message, and suggest prompts that feel natural while still driving your business goals.
Where do you usually film?
Common locations include downtown Oshkosh, near campus, and outside your storefront or event. We’ll recommend spots based on your audience and brand.
