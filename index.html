Authentic Content. Built by Sigma Pi.

Sigma Pi Creative Media is a student‑run content studio from the Gamma‑Mu Chapter at UW Oshkosh, focused on UGC and street‑interview content that feels real, not scripted.

Services

We design short‑form content specifically for TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts, with packages that match where your business is at—testing, growing, or scaling.

Starter UGC Package — $150–$250

For first‑time UGC or single promotions

A focused, low‑risk way to get your first piece of UGC live. We help you choose one clear message—like a new menu item, sale, or event—and build a short, punchy video around it.

  • 1 UGC video (15–30 seconds) filmed in Oshkosh
  • Basic editing, color, and sound cleanup
  • Simple on‑screen text or captions
  • Delivery formatted for one platform (TikTok, Reels, or Shorts)

Best if you’ve never done UGC before and want to see how it performs with your audience.

Growth Package — $300–$600

For local awareness and consistent posting

A mix of UGC and street‑interview content that gives you multiple angles from a single shoot. We capture both “talking to camera” style content and real reactions from people in Oshkosh.

  • 2–3 UGC videos (15–45 seconds each)
  • 1 street‑interview‑style video filmed on location
  • Hook‑driven intros and simple calls‑to‑action
  • Captions and light branding (logo, colors, or taglines)

Best if you want a small library of content to post over a few weeks and test what resonates.

Premium Campaign — $750–$1,500

For brands ready to run real campaigns

A full short‑form content stack designed to support a campaign, launch, or ongoing posting schedule. We plan multiple hooks, angles, and locations so you get variety from one project.

  • 3–5 UGC videos with multiple hooks and variations
  • Up to 2 street‑interview sessions in Oshkosh
  • Platform‑specific exports (TikTok, Reels, Shorts)
  • Basic posting cadence suggestions and content order

Best if you’re serious about short‑form content and want a consistent, on‑brand presence.

About Sigma Pi · Gamma‑Mu Chapter

Who We Are

Sigma Pi Fraternity was founded in 1897. The Gamma‑Mu Chapter at the University of Wisconsin–Oshkosh is an active part of campus life, with members involved in leadership, academics, and service.

Our chapter operates on the same values that guide our projects: show up prepared, communicate clearly, and represent our letters and partners with respect.

Our Values in the Work

We bring fraternity values—scholarship, leadership, service, and brotherhood—into how we work with local businesses. That means being on time, being honest about what we can deliver, and treating your brand like it’s our own.

As students, we live on the same platforms your customers use. As Sigma Pi members, we care about how we show up in the community. That combination is what drives Sigma Pi Creative Media.

Why It Matters for Your Business

You’re not hiring a random content creator—you’re partnering with a chapter that has a reputation on campus and in Oshkosh. Every project is a reflection of our letters and your brand at the same time.

The result: content that feels real, is built for the platforms that matter, and is created by people who actually live where your customers are.
